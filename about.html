About Us

LPX, Logical Procurement Excellence, is about one thing ensuring our customers are procuring their goods at the lowest cost bringing more profit dollars to their organization aiding them in being successful. We have no ties to any distributor or products, and we never will. Our one and only mission is to aid our customers in maximizing leverage and profit.

Our Team

Ray Schiffer Co-Founder, President, CEO

Ray oversees the entire organization while one of our co-founders, Rays career expands over 35 years in the food service industry holding many positions from delivery driver to President and CEO. While he has a clear understanding of the difficulty of running a restaurant on a daily basis, as well as the distributors trying to meet earnings.
